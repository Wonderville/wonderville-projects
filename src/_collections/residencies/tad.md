By [Doriencey](https://doriencey.itch.io/)

This is a small game made in pico8. 

Swim around a pond full of wonders and danger, where surviving can be a TAD difficult! This is a game about exploring the pond, eating and being eaten. If being eaten is not your thing and you just want to enjoy the atmosphere, chill mode might be for you.

The environment is highly reactive to you and your fellow pondmates, allowing for chaos to unfold, often leading to hilarious and unexpected situations. 

Other than food, there are six achievements to hunt down.

Some hints in case you're lost:

The other Tads sometimes give good advice, listen to them when you can.
